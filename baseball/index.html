Our Story

While our ballpark journey technically began with a visit to Busch Stadium in St. Louis in June 2009, the mission really took shape in 2012 when Josh's first anniversary gift to Samantha (paper, appropriately enough) was a baseball road trip featuring games in Milwaukee and Chicago. Somewhere between the highways, hotel rooms, and ballpark gates, a simple getaway turned into a quest. Over the next 16 years, baseball became woven into the fabric of our family, giving us countless shared memories, traditions, and adventures while cheering on our Braves across the country. What started as something Josh and Sam loved soon became something Ellie, Tilly, and Poppy loved too, each building their own ballpark counts and favorite baseball memories along the way.

We completed all 30 active MLB ballparks in June 2025, but the real accomplishment isn't the number; it's the time spent and memories made together. We were fortunate to experience a few historic parks before they closed, and with new stadiums already on the horizon, we're excited to keep the tradition alive and have our daughters carry the journey forward.
